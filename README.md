# My Favorite Movies
### Saving Private Ryan
*Saving Private Ryan* is a war movie directed by Steven Spielberg. It is a film that captured my pique interest in the history of the past world wars. It envelops the viewer into the scene of WWII that accurately depicts the experiences of soldiers even though it is not a true to life story.  
###  Interstellar
The story of *Interstellar* made me, as a viewer, to be enveloped by the mystery and complexity of space and the cosmos whilst connecting the story of family relationship within the film. 
###  Avengers Endgame
*Avengers Endgame* is one of the most critically acclaimed movie of Marvel studios and is one of the best movies I have watched due to its captivating visual effects and storyline. 
###  Forrest Gump
*Forrest Gump* is a classic tale of a simple man's journey through life,  love, and history. 
### Whiplash
*Whiplash* is a gripping drama about a young jazz drummer's intense mentorship under a ruthless instructor. 
### Django Unchained
*Django Unchained* is a western epic about a freed slave's quest for revenge and rescue in the pre-Civil war era. 
### The Mask
*The Mask* starred by Jim Carrey, is a comedic fantasy about a shy bank clerk who becomes a cartoon-like supervillain when wearing a magical mask.
### Catch Me If You Can
*Catch Me If You Can* is a biographical crime drama about the cat-and-mouse game between a con man and an FBI agent.
### Hacksaw Ridge
*Hacksaw Ridge* is a biographical war drama about a conscientious objector who becomes a hero in WWII. 
### Green Book
*Green Book* is a biographical drama about an unlikely friendship between on a black pianist and a white driver on a concert tour through the Deep South.
